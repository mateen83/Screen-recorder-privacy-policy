Privacy Policy

Screen Recorder is designed to allow users to record their screen, including tab, window, or full display, with optional microphone and system audio.

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

  • No personal information is collected
  • No screen recordings are uploaded to any server
  • No audio recordings are transmitted externally
  • No browsing history is tracked
  • No analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party monitoring tools are used

Local Processing

All screen and audio recordings are processed locally on your device using your browser’s built-in capabilities. Your recordings remain fully under your control and are never sent to external servers.

Permissions Usage

  • tabCapture / desktopCapture – Capture screen, tab, or window video
  • audioCapture – Capture microphone and system audio (if enabled)
  • downloads – Save recorded files (MP4) to your device
  • storage – Save user preferences (e.g., audio settings)
  • activeTab – Interact with the current tab when recording

Third-Party Access

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer any user data to third parties. No third-party services are integrated for data collection or processing.

Security

Since all data is processed locally, your recordings are not exposed to external risks associated with cloud storage or data transmission.
